Hello, my name is Jordan Gatewood. I go by Spirit on the internet. You may call me however you like. I am a 23 year old deaf man aspiring to be a game developer. Mainly because I want to make game about realistic air combat (not necessarily a simulator like Microsoft Flight simulator) and various others things that hasn't been made into a game.
